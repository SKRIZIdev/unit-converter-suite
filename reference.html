Common conversions

Length

  • 1 inch = 2.54 cm
  • 1 foot = 30.48 cm
  • 1 mile = 1.609 km
  • 1 metre = 3.281 ft

Weight

  • 1 lb = 0.4536 kg
  • 1 kg = 2.205 lb
  • 1 oz = 28.35 g
  • 1 stone = 6.35 kg

Temperature

  • 0 °C = 32 °F
  • 100 °C = 212 °F
  • °F = °C × 9/5 + 32
  • 37 °C = 98.6 °F

Volume

  • 1 gallon (US) = 3.785 L
  • 1 litre = 33.8 fl oz
  • 1 cup = 236.6 mL
  • 1 pint (US) = 473 mL
